Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis represents transformation, growth, and the journey of change.

Our TEDxStonehill event will explore diverse topics that reflect this theme, spanning areas like personal growth, societal change, technological evolution, and environmental impact. Expect an engaging line-up of speakers who will share their unique perspectives on change in our world.

Join us on November 26th at Stonehill for an inspiring day of talks, fully organized by our talented students. Experience firsthand the transformative power of new ideas and perspectives, brought to you by the next generation of thinkers.
